- **Topics**: Welcoming, menu recommendations, ordering specifics, preparation time, appetizers
- **Key Vocabulary**: مَطْعَم (restaurant), كَبْسَة (kabsa), سَلَطَة (salad), مُقَبِّلَات (appetizers), حُمُّص (hummus)

### 2. Job Interview (12 lines) - NEW TOPIC

- **Topics**: Work experience, achievements, motivation, strengths/weaknesses
- **Key Vocabulary**: خِبْرَة (experience), مُهَنْدِس (engineer), إِنْجَازَات (achievements), نِقَاطُ قُوَّة (strengths)
- **Grammar**: Past tense (عَمِلْتُ - I worked, قُدْتُ - I led), relative clauses, formal register

### 3. Discussing Travel Plans (12 lines) - NEW TOPIC
- **Context**: Detailed vacation planning with budget, destinations, and advice
- **Topics**: Travel destinations, booking, budget planning, tourist attractions
- **Key Vocabulary**: تُرْكِيَا (Turkey), حَجْز (booking), مِيزَانِيَّة (budget), آيَا صُوفْيَا (Hagia Sophia)
- **Grammar**: Future planning (سَأَبْقَى - I will stay), advice (أَنْصَحُكَ - I advise you), past experience

### 4. At the Doctor - Detailed Consultation (12 lines) - ENHANCED
- **Context**: Comprehensive medical consultation with symptoms, diagnosis, and treatment plan
- **Topics**: Detailed symptoms, medical history, diagnosis, prescription, recovery timeline
- **Key Vocabulary**: أَلَم (pain), مَعِدَة (stomach), غَثَيَان (nausea), الْتِهَاب (inflammation), نِظَام غِذَائِيّ (diet)

### 5. Discussing Education and Dreams (12 lines) - NEW TOPIC

### 6. Family Gathering Discussion (12 lines) - NEW TOPIC

- **Topics**: Missing family, planning gatherings, venue selection, preparations
- **Key Vocabulary**: لِقَاء عَائِلِيّ (family gathering), عِيد الْفِطْر (Eid al-Fitr), جَدَّة (grandmother), تَرْتِيبَات (arrangements)
- **Grammar**: Time expressions (مُنْذُ - since), emotional expressions (اشْتَقْتُ - I missed)

### 7. Discussing Current Events and Society (12 lines) - NEW TOPIC

- **Topics**: Technology advancement, social media addiction, balance, digital education

- **Grammar**: Opinion expressions (أَعْتَقِدُ - I think), agreement (أُوَافِقُكَ - I agree with you), metaphors (سِلَاح ذُو حَدَّيْنِ - double-edged sword)

- **Grammar**: Abstract concepts, philosophical expressions, rhetorical questions, wisdom statements

## Advanced Language Features

### Complex Grammar Structures
- **Past tense verbs**: عَمِلْتُ (I worked), قُدْتُ (I led), زُرْتُ (I visited)
- **Future tense**: سَأَبْقَى (I will stay), سَأَتَّصِلُ (I will call), سَيَكُونُ (it will be)
- **Superlatives**: الأَشْهَرُ (most famous), الأَلَذُّ (most delicious), أَهَمّ (most important)
- **Relative clauses**: الَّذِي تُفَضِّلُهُ (that you prefer), الَّتِي يَجِبُ (that must)
- **Conditional phrases**: إِنْ شَاءَ اللهُ (God willing), بِإِذْنِ اللهِ (with God's permission)
- **Causation**: لِأَنَّ (because), يُفَسِّرُ (explains), يَرْتَبِطُ (is connected to)
- **Comparison**: أَفْضَل (better), أَهَمّ (more important), أَغْلَى (most precious)

### Rich Vocabulary Domains
- **Professional**: خِبْرَة (experience), شَرِكَة (company), إِنْجَازَات (achievements)
- **Medical**: أَلَم (pain), أَعْرَاض (symptoms), الْتِهَاب (inflammation), دَوَاء (medicine)
- **Academic**: جَامِعَة (university), تَخَصُّص (specialization), دِرَاسَات عُلْيَا (graduate studies)
- **Travel**: حَجْز (booking), مِيزَانِيَّة (budget), فُنْدُق (hotel), طَيَرَان (flight)
- **Technology**: تَكْنُولُوجِيَا (technology), هَوَاتِف (phones), تَرْبِيَة رَقْمِيَّة (digital education)
- **Philosophy**: سَعَادَة (happiness), نَجَاح (success), رِضَا (contentment), حِكْمَة (wisdom)

### Natural Dialogue Features
- **Follow-up questions**: Building on previous responses
- **Emotional expressions**: اشْتَقْتُ (I missed), سُعَدَاء (happy)
- **Advice giving**: أَنْصَحُكَ (I advise you), يَجِبُ (must)
- **Agreement/disagreement**: أُوَافِقُكَ (I agree), صَحِيحٌ (true)
- **Idiomatic expressions**: مَا شَاءَ اللهُ, جَزَاكَ اللهُ خَيْرًا
- **Formal vs informal register**: Professional interview vs casual friends
