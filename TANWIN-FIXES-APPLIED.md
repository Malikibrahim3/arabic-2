## Why This Matters

### Educational Benefits:
1. **Consistency**: What learners see matches what they hear
2. **Grammar**: Teaches proper Arabic case endings (i'rab)
3. **Reading Skills**: Prepares learners for real Arabic texts
4. **Pronunciation**: Helps learners pronounce words correctly

### Tanwīn Rules Applied:
- **Dammatan (ٌ)**: Nominative case (subject) - most common
- **Fathatan (ً)**: Accusative case (object)
- **Kasratan (ٍ)**: Genitive case (after prepositions)

### Special Cases:
- **Definite words** (with ال): No tanwīn (e.g., الكتاب not الكتابٌ)
- **Feminine taa marbouta** (ة): Takes tanwīn (e.g., مَدْرَسَةٌ)
- **Alif maqsura** (ى): No tanwīn (e.g., مُسْتَشْفَى)

## Impact on Learning

Students will now:
- ✅ See proper grammatical Arabic

- ✅ Understand case endings naturally
