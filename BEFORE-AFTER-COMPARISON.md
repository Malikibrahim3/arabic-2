## 🔥 The "Duolingo Effect"

### What Makes Duolingo Addictive?

1. **Constant Review** - You never forget what you learned
2. **Varied Exercises** - Same content, different ways
3. **Progressive Difficulty** - Always challenging, never overwhelming
4. **Visible Progress** - You see yourself improving

### How We Achieved It:

1. ✅ **Constant Review** - Refreshers in EVERY lesson
2. ✅ **Varied Exercises** - Recognition, audio, assembly, translation
3. ✅ **Progressive Difficulty** - Gradual increase with maintained review
4. ✅ **Visible Progress** - Students see old content becoming easy
