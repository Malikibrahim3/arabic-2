# Unit 7: Real Conversations 🗣️

## Overview
Unit 7 is the culmination of the entire Arabic learning journey! Students apply everything they've learned in realistic, practical conversations.

## Structure
- **6 Conversation Nodes** + **1 Final Test**
- **4 Rounds per conversation**
- **Multiple exercise types** per conversation

## Conversations Included

### 1. Meeting Someone New 👋
**Context:** Two people meet for the first time
**Speakers:** أَحْمَد (Ahmad) & فَاطِمَة (Fatima)
**Key Phrases:**
- اَلسَّلامُ عَلَيْكُم (Peace be upon you)
- مَا اسْمُكِ؟ (What is your name?)
- اِسْمِي... (My name is...)
- تَشَرَّفْنَا (Nice to meet you)

### 2. How Are You? 😊
**Context:** Friends greeting each other
**Speakers:** عَلِي (Ali) & مَرْيَم (Maryam)
**Key Phrases:**
- صَبَاحُ الخَيْرِ (Good morning)
- كَيْفَ حَالُكِ؟ (How are you?)
- أَنَا بِخَيْرٍ (I am fine)
- الحَمْدُ لِلّهِ (Praise be to God)

### 3. At Home 🏠
**Context:** Talking about family and home
**Speakers:** خَالِد (Khalid), سَارَة (Sarah), الأَب (Father), الأُم (Mother)
**Key Phrases:**
- هَذَا بَيْتِي (This is my house)
- بَيْتُكَ كَبِيرٌ (Your house is big)
- هَذَا أَبِي وَهَذِهِ أُمِّي (This is my father and this is my mother)
- أَهْلًا وَسَهْلًا (Welcome)

### 4. At the Market 🛒
**Context:** Shopping for food
**Speakers:** زَبُون (Customer) & بَائِع (Seller)
**Key Phrases:**
- مَاذَا تُرِيدُ؟ (What do you want?)
- أُرِيدُ خُبْزًا وَحَلِيبًا (I want bread and milk)
- كَمْ الثَّمَنُ؟ (How much is the price?)
- شُكْرًا (Thank you)

### 5. Asking for Help 🗺️
**Context:** Someone needs directions
**Speakers:** سَائِح (Tourist) & رَجُل (Man)
**Key Phrases:**
- عَفْوًا، أَيْنَ المَسْجِدُ؟ (Excuse me, where is the mosque?)
- هَلْ تَتَكَلَّمُ الإِنْجِلِيزِيَّةَ؟ (Do you speak English?)
- أَنَا لا أَفْهَمُ (I don't understand)
- لا بَأْسَ (No problem)

### 6. Saying Goodbye 👋
**Context:** Friends parting ways
**Speakers:** يُوسُف (Yusuf) & لَيْلَى (Layla)
**Key Phrases:**
- أَنَا ذَاهِبٌ الآنَ (I am going now)
- إِلَى أَيْنَ؟ (Where to?)
- مَعَ السَّلامَةِ (Goodbye)
- اللهُ مَعَكِ (God be with you)

## Learning Progression

### Round 1: Learn the Conversation
- Introduction cards for each line
- Listen to native pronunciation
- See Arabic text with full voweling
- Read English translations

### Round 2: Comprehension
- Match speakers to their lines
- Identify what was said
- Audio-based questions
- Multiple choice format

### Round 3: Build Sentences
- Sentence assembly exercises
- Reconstruct conversation lines
- Mix of comprehension and assembly
- Practice word order

### Round 4: Master the Dialogue
- Match pairs (speaker to line, Arabic to English)
- Mixed review of all skills
- Comprehensive understanding check
- Full conversation mastery

## Exercise Types Used

1. **Introduction Cards** - Learn each line with audio
2. **Multiple Choice** - Comprehension and translation
3. **Sentence Assembly** - Build conversation lines
4. **Match Pairs** - Connect speakers, Arabic, and English
5. **Trap Select** - Distinguish similar phrases
6. **Audio Exercises** - Listen and identify

## Final Test

The Unit 7 Final Test includes:
- Translation from all 6 conversations
- Sentence assembly from all dialogues
- Match pairs across conversations
- Comprehensive conversation mastery check

Unit 7 teaches important cultural aspects:
- **Islamic greetings** (السلام عليكم)

- **Hospitality phrases** (أهلاً وسهلاً)
- **Polite requests** (تفضل/تفضلي)
- **Proper farewells** (الله معك)
