## 🎓 Realistic Expectations

### Most Likely Scenario:
**Average learner, 1 hour/day = 6-8 weeks (1.5-2 months)**

### Why This Is Realistic:
1. **Spaced repetition built-in** - Duolingo-style refreshers
2. **Immediate retry system** - Mistakes are retried 4 exercises later
3. **Cumulative tests** - Force review of old material
4. **Progressive difficulty** - Builds on previous knowledge
5. **Audio + visual** - Multiple learning modalities

### Factors That Speed Up Learning:
- ✅ Previous Arabic exposure
- ✅ Familiarity with other languages
- ✅ Strong phonetic awareness
- ✅ Consistent daily practice
- ✅ Using audio actively (not just reading)

### Factors That Slow Down Learning:
- ⚠️ No previous Arabic exposure
- ⚠️ First time learning a non-Latin script
- ⚠️ Inconsistent practice (skipping days)
- ⚠️ Rushing through exercises
- ⚠️ Not using audio
