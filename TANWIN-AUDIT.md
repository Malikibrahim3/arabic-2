## Notes
- Tanwīn marks: ً (fathatan), ٌ (dammatan), ٍ (kasratan)
- Feminine words ending in ة (taa marbouta) take tanwīn: ةٌ
- Words in definite state (with ال) don't take tanwīn
- Proper nouns typically don't take tanwīn
