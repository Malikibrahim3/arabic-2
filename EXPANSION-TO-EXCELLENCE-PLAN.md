### Phase 4: Expand Unit 6 Sentences (10 → 30)

#### Add 20 New Sentences:

**Questions (5 sentences):**
1. مَا اسْمُكَ؟ (Maa ismuka?) - What is your name?
2. أَيْنَ تَسْكُنُ؟ (Ayna taskunu?) - Where do you live?
3. كَمْ عُمْرُكَ؟ (Kam 'umruka?) - How old are you?
4. مَاذَا تَعْمَلُ؟ (Maadhaa ta'malu?) - What do you do?
5. هَلْ تَتَكَلَّمُ العَرَبِيَّةَ؟ (Hal tatakallamu al-'arabiyyah?) - Do you speak Arabic?

**Negations (5 sentences):**
6. لَا أَفْهَمُ (Laa afham) - I don't understand
7. لَيْسَ عِنْدِي (Laysa 'indee) - I don't have
8. لَمْ أَذْهَبْ (Lam adhhab) - I didn't go
9. لَا أَعْرِفُ (Laa a'rifu) - I don't know
10. لَيْسَ هُنَا (Laysa hunaa) - It's not here

**Commands (5 sentences):**
11. تَعَالَ هُنَا (Ta'aala hunaa) - Come here
12. اِجْلِسْ (Ijlis) - Sit down

14. اِذْهَبْ (Idhhab) - Go
15. اِسْمَعْ (Isma') - Listen

**Descriptions (5 sentences):**
16. الطَّقْسُ جَمِيلٌ (At-taqsu jameelun) - The weather is beautiful
17. الكِتَابُ كَبِيرٌ (Al-kitaabu kabeerun) - The book is big
18. البَيْتُ نَظِيفٌ (Al-baytu nadheefun) - The house is clean
19. الطَّعَامُ لَذِيذٌ (At-ta'aamu ladheethun) - The food is delicious
